Support & FAQ

Everything you need to know about our field-grade equipment, secure logistics, and professional services.

Ordering & Payments

Which payment methods do you accept?
We support all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), UPI, and secure Net Banking. For verified EMS agencies, we also offer Net-30 purchase order terms.
Can I modify an order after it is placed?
Because we aim for rapid field deployment, orders enter our fulfillment queue within 2 hours. If you need to make changes, please contact support immediately with your Order ID.

Shipping & Logistics

How fast is domestic shipping?
Most domestic orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while Express Deployment shipping takes 1-2 business days.
Do you ship to APO/FPO addresses?
Yes. As a dedicated EMS supplier, we proudly ship to all military APO, FPO, and DPO addresses worldwide to support our deployed responders.

Returns & Warranty

What is your professional return policy?
We offer a 30-day "Field-Ready" guarantee. Unused and non-sterile items can be returned for a full refund. Note that opened medical consumables cannot be returned due to biohazard safety protocols.
Do stethoscopes come with a warranty?
Yes. Our premium diagnostic lines include a 5-year manufacturer warranty covering acoustic performance and tubing integrity. Select "Cardiology" models include lifetime replacement parts.

Agency & Bulk Orders

Do you offer discounts for entire departments?
Absolutely. We provide tiered volume pricing for EMS agencies, Fire Departments, and Hospital systems. Contact our Sales team for a customized bulk quotation.

Security & Privacy

How is my personal data handled?
We utilize AES-256 bank-level encryption for all data at rest. We never store full credit card details on our servers, and we never sell your professional data to third-party advertisers.
