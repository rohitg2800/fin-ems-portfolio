Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about orders, shipping, returns, warranties, and bulk EMS gear.

Ordering & Payments

How to place orders, pay securely, and track your purchase.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We support credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay, Amex), UPI, and net banking. Approved agencies can also request purchase-order terms for bulk orders.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be changed or cancelled within a short window before they enter the packing queue. Contact support@emsluxe.com or use live chat with your order ID as soon as possible.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through PCI‑DSS Level 1 compliant gateways using TLS 1.3 encryption. We never store full card details on our servers.

Shipping & Delivery

Timelines, tracking, and international options.

How long does shipping usually take?

Most in‑stock domestic orders ship within 48 hours and arrive in 3–7 business days. Express and international options are available at checkout with estimated delivery windows.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to most regions worldwide. Duties or import taxes may be charged by your local customs office depending on your country’s regulations.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking link by email and SMS (where available). You can also view tracking information on your account order history page.

Returns & Warranty

What’s covered, what isn’t, and how to start a return.

What is your return policy?

Unused, non‑sterile items can be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in resellable condition with original packaging and contents included.

How do I start a return?

Email returns@emsluxe.com with your order ID and the item you’d like to return. We’ll send a prepaid label for eligible domestic orders or guide you to the nearest return hub.

Do your products include a warranty?

Most tools include a 12‑month warranty against manufacturing defects. Some premium lines (like select stethoscopes and shears) offer extended or lifetime replacement coverage.

Agencies, Schools & Bulk Orders

Support for EMS agencies, hospitals, and training programs.

Do you offer bulk or agency pricing?

Yes. Tiered discounts typically start at 10 units per SKU, with deeper discounts for agency‑wide outfitting. Contact sales@emsluxe.com with your gear list for a custom quote.

Can you build custom kits for our department or school?

We regularly build custom trauma, airway, and student starter kits for fire/EMS agencies and education programs. Send your checklist or syllabus, and we’ll propose an itemized kit.

Do you accept purchase orders?

Verified agencies, hospitals, and accredited schools can be approved for purchase‑order terms after a short account review. Email ap@emsluxe.com to start the process.

Products & Sizing

Choosing the right model, fit, and configuration.

Which stethoscope is best for EMT or paramedic students?

Our Classic Acoustic and Lightweight Student models are optimized for training labs and early ride‑alongs. For advanced cardiac work, consider the Cardiology Pro line.

How do I choose the right size for pants or apparel?

Each apparel item includes a detailed size guide on its product page. When between sizes, we recommend sizing up to allow for layering and movement onscene.

Are your trauma kits supplied with medications or IV fluids?

No. Due to regulatory requirements, we only provide hardware and consumables such as bandages and tools. Medications and IV fluids must be sourced through appropriate medical supply channels.

Security & Privacy

How we handle your data and keep your account safe.

How is my personal data protected?

We encrypt account data at rest and in transit. We never sell personal data, and we honor GDPR/CCPA requests to export or delete your profile when submitted through our privacy portal.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact privacy@emsluxe.com from your registered email address, and we’ll guide you through verification and permanent account deletion.

How do I contact support if my question isn’t listed here?

You can reach us anytime at support@emsluxe.com, via the contact form on the Trust page, or through live chat during business hours.
